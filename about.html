About CODE Club

Coding with Digital Experience is a vibrant Code Club initiative, founded at the African Leadership University (ALU), dedicated to empowering high school students across Rwanda. Our club is built on the belief that digital literacy and hands-on coding experience are essential skills for today’s youth. By providing a collaborative and inclusive environment, we aim to inspire young minds to confidently embrace technology, develop essential coding skills, and pursue advanced careers in tech.

Our Mission

To bridge the digital literacy gap and foster a generation of tech-savvy students who are equipped with practical coding skills, problem-solving abilities, and collaborative mindsets. We strive to create an impactful learning experience that goes beyond the classroom, empowering students to take initiative in their tech journeys.

Our Vision

By 2026, we aim to reach at least 30 high schools across Rwanda, with a minimum of 60% female participation in each school’s club. Our goal is to drive long-lasting change by empowering young women and fostering a culture of digital confidence and inclusion.

Our Core Principles

  • Inclusion and Diversity: We prioritize accessibility and aim to empower girls and underserved communities through a balanced and inclusive club structure.
  • Peer Learning: Our club thrives on a collaborative spirit, with members teaching and supporting each other to reach new milestones.
  • Practical Skills: Our focus is on real-world applications, ensuring students can confidently tackle tech challenges and understand the value of digital skills.

Impact on Students

Students who join CODE Club gain:

  • Confidence in coding, enabling them to pursue advanced technology concepts.
  • A strong foundation in digital literacy, preparing them for tech-driven careers.
  • The chance to engage in a community that supports their growth and development.

Support Needed

To continue and expand our impact, we seek financial and logistical support for transportation, materials, and event organization. Our ambition to reach underserved regions requires grants, sponsorships, and partnerships that will enable us to extend our programming beyond the ALU campus to high schools nationwide.
